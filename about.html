About Me

Let me tell you a few things...

BIO

I am a classically-trained singer, composer and musician from Surrey. I perform and compose a wide variety of music, from popular to classical I am a saxophonist and play the piano, which enables me to accompany myself to a number of songs. Alternatively backing tracks/other musicians are sometimes necessary depending on the music. I am currently studying Music and Music Technology at the University of Nottingham, where I am also the Musical Director of the A Capella group Radio Octave as well as conductor of various choirs within the University's music department. In 2016 I was Assistant Musical Director of my school's production of Grease, in which I conducted the band and cast for the first act of the show, as well as taking rehearsals.


I am generally available for performances in the London, the south-east and East Midlands areas. I would be delighted to speak to you about your event and requirements. Please do contact me for further details on availability and rates. Thank you!
