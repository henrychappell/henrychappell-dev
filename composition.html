BIO

I have been composing and arranging music since I was 15 and continue to do so as a hobby, as well as for commission. Most of my current arrangements are for A Capella groups, though in the past I arranged music for internal school events. Although I enjoy composing/arranging in a number of different styles, my specialism is in choral music. I can compose on-demand for an event, and also arrange existing songs and compositions in a tailored context.
