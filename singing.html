BIO

I am a classically-trained baritone, having taken professional singing lessons since the age of 10. I have been a member of the National Youth Choirs of Great Britain since the age of 12, and am currently a member of the Main Choir. Being a member of this organisation has allowed me to perform in some of the country’s best venues and occasions, most recently in the BBC’s 2018 First Night of the Proms at the Royal Albert Hall, whilst elsewhere I have sung at Leeds Castle, the O2 arena and toured with choirs to France, Belgium and the Czech Republic. As a soloist I have performed in numerous school and public concerts and have wide experience of singing at private occasions such as funerals and weddings. I would be very happy to adapt performances according to audience preference.
